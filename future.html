Roadmap to Clinical Impact

We are advancing with a clear roadmap to clinical entry and positioned to lead a new era of precision CNS therapeutics.

Neuro11 is advancing with a clear roadmap to clinical entry, driven by our commitment to bringing transformative therapies to patients in need. Our strategic development plan combines rigorous scientific validation with efficient clinical progression.

Candidate Selection: Finalize our lead HDAC11 inhibitor candidate through comprehensive preclinical evaluation

IND-Enabling Studies: Complete all required safety, toxicology, and pharmacology studies for regulatory submission

IND Application: Submit our investigational new drug application to initiate Phase 1 clinical trials

Long-Term Vision

Platform Expansion: Extend our HDAC11 inhibitor platform into Parkinson's disease, chronic pain, and oncology indications

Strategic Partnerships: Establish collaborations with leading pharmaceutical companies and research institutions

Global Impact: Scale our technology to address unmet medical needs across multiple therapeutic areas

With scalable technology, strong intellectual property, and a differentiated mechanism of action, we are uniquely positioned to revolutionize the treatment of neurological disorders. Our vision extends beyond single indications to establishing a new paradigm in precision CNS medicine.

Our Commitment

We are committed to advancing breakthrough therapies that address critical unmet needs in neurology, bringing hope to patients and families affected by devastating CNS disorders.
